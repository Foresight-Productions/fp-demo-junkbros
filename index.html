FAQ

Common Questions.

How much does junk removal cost in Yakima?

Pricing is based on the volume of space your items take in our truck. The minimum load fee is $125 and a full truck load is $545, with multiple tiers in between. All prices include labor, tax, and disposal fees.

Do you offer same-day junk removal?

Yes! We offer same-day service across Yakima Valley. Text us a photo of your items and we'll get you a quote fast — most responses within 20 minutes.

What items do you take?

Almost everything — household junk, appliances, furniture, hot tubs, mattresses, yard waste, construction debris, estate cleanouts, and more. Not sure? Just text us a photo.

How do I get a free quote?

Call or text (509) 314-8132 with a photo of what you need hauled. We provide a free no-obligation on-site quote before any work begins — no pressure, no hidden fees.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Yakima, Union Gap, Selah, West Valley, Terrace Heights, Moxee, Wapato, Sunnyside, Zillah, Grandview, Toppenish, and Prosser, WA.

Are prices all-inclusive?

Yes. All quoted prices include labor, tax, and disposal fees. No surprise charges after the job. Special items like hot tubs, pianos, and pool tables have set surcharges listed on our pricing page.
